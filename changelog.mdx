## Upgrade to the Latest Version

Follow these steps to ensure you have the most recent Snow AI features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Current Version" icon="check-circle">

  Log in to your dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com` and navigate to Settings > About.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Update CLI" icon="download">

````bash
npm update @snowai/cli@latest
# or
pip install --upgrade snowai
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Services" icon="refresh-cw">

  Run `snowai restart` to apply changes.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify" icon="shield">

  Test a prompt: `snowai prompt "Build a landing page"`.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
After upgrading, review your prompts for compatibility with new AI chaining features.
</Callout>
