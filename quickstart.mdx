<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An available domain name (optional for preview)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for Snow AI to access the dashboard and AI tools. The process takes under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.snowai.app/signup` and enter your email and password.

    Choose a strong password and agree to the terms.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a business email for professional workflows.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Snow AI.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access Dashboard and Select Template

Once verified, log in to generate your first site.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.snowai.app/login`.

    Enter your credentials to access the main dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Template" icon="layout">
    Browse the template library categorized by industry (e.g., eCommerce, Portfolio).

    Select a starter template like "Modern Business" to begin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate Website with AI Prompts

Snow AI uses natural language prompts to customize your site instantly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Simple Prompt" icon="sparkles">
    In the AI builder, enter a prompt like:

````text
Create a landing page for a coffee shop with menu, contact form, and booking calendar. Use warm colors and modern design.
````

    Click "Generate" to build the site.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Advanced Prompt" icon="cpu">
    For more control:

````text
Build an eCommerce homepage for Snow AI tools. Include hero section with CTA, feature cards for AI website builder and VPS hosting, testimonials, and pricing table. Brand color: #673DE6. Add CRM integration.
````

    Review and regenerate sections as needed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Publish and Preview

Finalize and launch your site.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview Site" icon="eye">
    Use the live preview to test responsiveness on desktop and mobile.

    Edit content directly in the visual editor.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Connect a domain or use the free subdomain (e.g., `yoursite.snowai.app`).

    Click "Publish" to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! Your first AI-generated website is live. Monitor analytics in the dashboard.
</Callout>
