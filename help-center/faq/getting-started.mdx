## Quick Steps

1. Sign in at `https://dashboard.snow-ai.com`
2. Click "New Project"
3. Enter a name and description
4. Select a workspace (optional)
5. Click "Create"

Your project appears in the dashboard. Use it to organize models, datasets, and tasks.

## Tip

<Callout kind="tip">You can move or rename projects anytime under Project Settings.</Callout>
