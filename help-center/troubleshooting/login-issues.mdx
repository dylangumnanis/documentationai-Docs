---
title: Can't log in to Snow AI
description: Fix login problems with Snow AI.
---

## Symptoms

- Login fails with error: "Invalid credentials"
- Password reset emails not received

## Fix Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email and password">
    Ensure you use the correct email registered with Snow AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and follow instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email support@snow-ai.com if problems persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
