---
title: Creating a Workspace in Snow AI
description: Guide to set up a new workspace.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Workspaces">
    Log in and select "Workspaces" from the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new workspace">
    Click "+ New Workspace", enter a name, and click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Use the "Members" tab to add teammates by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
