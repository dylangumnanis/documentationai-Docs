## Overview

Snow AI empowers you to build stunning websites using artificial intelligence, without needing coding expertise. You simply describe your vision through natural language prompts, and Snow AI generates complete sites with design, content, CRM integrations, and booking forms. This platform streamlines your online presence, making it ideal for businesses, freelancers, and individuals launching quickly.

Whether you start from scratch or enhance existing projects, Snow AI handles the heavy lifting so you focus on your brand.

## Quick Start

Get your first site live in under 10 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create a free account at [https://app.snowai.app](https://app.snowai.app).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prompt Your Site" icon="message-circle">
    Describe your site: "Build a portfolio for a photographer with gallery and contact form."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize" icon="edit-3">
    Tweak AI-generated elements using the visual editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch" icon="rocket">
    Publish to a custom domain or Snow AI hosting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
  ```bash
  curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/websites \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{
      "prompt": "Modern business landing page",
      "features": ["contact-form", "gallery"]
    }'
  ```
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/websites', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
      'Content-Type': 'application/json',
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      prompt: 'Modern business landing page',
      features: ['contact-form', 'gallery']
    })
  });
  const site = await response.json();
  ```
